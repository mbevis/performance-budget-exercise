Food & Dining

Barcocina's

1629 Thames Street, Baltimore, MD 21231 (410) 563-1500

From the group behind Bond Street Social and executive chef Marc Dixon, Barcocina, Baltimore's newest restaurant and social scene in Fell's Point waterfront.

The restaurant features Mexican inspired cuisine with fresh ingredients and original twists meant to share amongst friends. Barcocina's cocktail list emphasize fresh squeezed juices for next level freshness and taste. With amazing outdoor space and uninhibited views of the harbor, Barcocina is a premier place to eat, drink and socialize. The beautiful and fully renovated space features glass and aluminum garage door walls that open to the harbor, so every seat indoor and outdoor will make the beautiful harbor view a part of the Barcocina experience.

Bertha's Muscles

View Photo

View Photo

734 S. Broadway, Baltimore, MD 21231 (410) 327-5795

For over 40 years, as far as Baltimore restaurants are concerned, Bertha’s has set the standard for charm in “Charm City”. Established in 1972, we have continuously provided restaurant customers in Baltimore, Maryland with the best crab cakes, and our one of a kind delivery of our house specialty, mussels.

Recognized for our world famous slogan “Eat Bertha’s Mussels”, we have been serving Baltimore residents and visitors alike in our Fell’s Point location consistently for over two generations.”

The historic district of Fell’s Point was the original Baltimore Harbor, and Bertha’s was the first sit down dining room in Fell’s Point.Bertha's We are proud to be one of the original restaurants associated with Baltimore Restaurant Week and support all that is Charm City. Visitors to the now popular Inner Harbor will find a pleasant trip on the Water taxi to Fell’s Point topped off with a bowl of mussels and a pint of Bertha’s Best Bitter a perfect way to spend your time here in Baltimore. Bertha’s also offers private group settings with preset menu options. We take reservations seven days a week for our eclectic dining rooms and bar and are always available to suggest places to stay and sights to see. We look forward to greeting you here at Bertha’s!

The Black Olive

View Photo

View Photo

814 S. Bond Street, Baltimore, MD 21231 (410) 276-7141

The Black Olive Restaurant sits in quaint elegance on the cobblestone section of Bond Street in old Fells Point, and though the restaurant itself is only a few years old, the spirit from which it springs holds a story that stretches through multiple generations. The restaurant is owned by the Spiliadis family, whose passion for food as art is grounded in a basic aesthetic principle: cook your food with an eye towards simplicity and tradition, and use only the finest ingredients, no matter what it takes to find them.

Kooper's Tavern

1702 Thames Street, Baltimore, MD 21231 (410) 563-5423

Kooper was our Yellow Lab who was born on January 14, 1995. At the early stages of puppyhood, he contracted parvo (a very difficult disease to overcome). He beat the odds and lived until May 2nd 2007. His friendship and loyalty were second to none and now his legacy will live on forever. It was, and is my mission as the restaurant owner to make sure all of our guests have fun at Kooper’s. Woody his brother, our black lab, has a restaurant two doors down above our Irish Pub… Sláinte. He too has gone to that place above full of biscuits and tennis balls. The two of them have once again been reunited and now look down upon us. We have added three new members to our family; Haywood our black lab, Leon (lee-awn) our little French doggie (a petit basset griffon vendeen) and Ronan our yellow lab pup!

As he wanted it, we built Kooper’s Tavern on the reputation of great food (he loved to eat) with excellent service and plenty of libations to go around. If you are looking for a place to host a party, no matter what the occasion, we will make it the perfect place..
